## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Severity | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |

| T-02-02 | Denial of Service | payload de `admin:start-phase` | medium | mitigate | Validació `phase ∈ {html,css,js}` i `durationMs` numèric positiu abans de mutar; handler embolcallat en try/catch. |
